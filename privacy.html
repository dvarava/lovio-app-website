Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy governs your use of the Lovio application, provided by SIA FOURPLEX ("we", "us", or "our").

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when using Lovio, including your account details, profile information, partner connection IDs, relationship milestones, notes, drawings, and usage data.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information to provide, maintain, and improve features like date idea generation (powered by AI), partner syncing, push notifications, and analytics.

3. AI Features

Our "Generate Date" feature utilizes third-party AI models. We send anonymized context (such as location data) to provide appropriate date suggestions, ensuring no personally identifiable information (PII) is included in prompts.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. Data is shared with your explicitly linked partner. We may share anonymized, aggregated data with analytics platforms to improve the app.

5. Data Deletion & Contact

You can request account deletion at any time within the app settings or by emailing support@getlovio.app. We will process your request within 48 hours.
